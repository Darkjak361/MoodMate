# MoodMate Privacy Policy - 100% Professional Capstone 🛡️⚖️✨

This Privacy Policy describes how **MoodMate** handles your data. As a mental health companion, your privacy is our **100,000,000% Priority.**

## 🔍 1. Data Collection
MoodMate is designed with a **Privacy-First** approach. 🧘
*   **Mood Awareness**: We collect the text you enter for AI sentiment analysis to provide empathetic feedback.
*   **Authentication**: We store your name, email, and a securely hashed password (using **Bcrypt**) for account security.

## 🎤 2. Voice & Microphone Usage
MoodMate uses a **"Smart-Mic"** strategy to ensure 100% privacy:
*   We **NEVER** record or store your audio on our servers. 🔒
*   Voice-to-text is processed locally on your device via the System Keyboard (iPhone/Android) or the Web Speech API (Web Browser).

## 🧹 3. Privacy-First Erasure (100% Cleaning)
To guarantee your confidentiality, we have implemented an **Automatic Data Cleaning** protocol:
*   **Logout Erasure**: Every time you log out, your **ENTIRE** mood history and all AI insights are permanently erased from our database. ⚓️🛡️
*   **Account Deletion**: Deleting your account permanently wipes all your records, settings, and credentials from our system. This cannot be undone.

## 🌗 4. Local Persistence
We use **Secure Local Storage** (@react-native-async-storage/async-storage) to save your theme preferences (Light/Dark mode) on your device for a 100% seamless experience.

## 🛡️ 5. Security Protocols
*   All data transmitted between the app and the server is protected via **Secure Tunnels**.
*   Passwords are never stored in plain text.
*   We use **Hugging Face AI (Inference API)** for sentiment analysis; your text is processed only for the moment of analysis and is not used to train global models.

---
**MoodMate: Your Privacy is 100% Protected.** 🏆🥇🎉👋🏮✨🕺🌟
